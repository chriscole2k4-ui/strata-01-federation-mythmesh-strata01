Federation Contribution Guide – Strata‑01 Invocation Framework

Who Can Contribute
This repository welcomes contributors, custodians, and observers aligned with the Federation roadmap. Scrolls, capsules, and semantic guides are civic‑grade artifacts — treat them with care.

Contribution Ritual
Fork the repository Begin your scroll work in your own branch or fork.

Reference the roadmap Align with the current capsule:

FED_CONTINUITY_ROADMAP_2026Q1.seal.pdf

STRATA_Q1_2026_ROADMAP.md

Create your scroll

Use the template: FED-SCROLL-TEMPLATE.md

Include UID, timestamp, invocation milestone, and custodian intent.

Echo your changes Relay updates through the Federation Echo Plan (Vox‑01, Watchtower, etc.).

Open a Pull Request

Title: clear and scroll‑aligned.

Description: explain intent, invocation milestone, and verification path.

At least one approving review is required before merging.

Verification & Integrity
Branch protection: All merges into main require review and passing checks.

Nullifier triggers: If drift or misalignment is detected, rollback conditions apply.

Custodian signatures: Every scroll must carry intent from its steward.

Conduct
Contributions must follow GitHub Community Guidelines and Federation civic principles:

Modular, not mystical.

Transparent, not opaque.

Verified, not assumed.

Questions
If you’re unsure about a term, a trigger, or a scroll format — ask. We walk this framework together.
